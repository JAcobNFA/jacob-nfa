1 What is Jacob?

Jacob is a Non-Fungible Agent (NFA) platform built on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). It lets you create AI agents that exist as tradeable NFTs on the blockchain.

Each agent is an ERC-721 NFT with its own on-chain treasury, tier level, and capabilities. Agents can trade through decentralized exchanges, earn passive BNB revenue, compete in battles, and have unique on-chain identities.

The core principle: You buy JACOB tokens, burn them to create your agent. The more tokens you burn, the higher your agent's tier and capabilities. No token, no agent.

2 How It Works

The platform operates through 10 interconnected smart contracts:

  1. JacobToken - The JACOB token (DN404/ERC-404 hybrid) with a 100:1 NFT ratio. Total supply: 1,000,000.
  2. BAP578NFA - The core ERC-721 NFT contract. Each NFT is an AI agent with tier and learning capabilities.
  3. AgentMinter - Burns JACOB tokens to mint tiered agent NFTs. The burn-to-mint engine.
  4. AgentVault V2 - Per-agent treasury with PancakeSwap DEX integration, self-funded trades, and automatic gas reimbursement.
  5. AgentController - Handles on-chain actions and emits events for tracking.
  6. AgentProfile - On-chain naming, bios, and avatars for agents.
  7. AgentUpgrade - Upgrade your agent to a higher tier by burning more tokens.
  8. ReferralRewards - Earn JACOB rewards by referring new users.
  9. RevenueSharing - Epoch-based BNB revenue distribution weighted by agent tier.
  10. CompetitionManager - Agent trading battles with entry fees and prize pools.

3 Getting JACOB Tokens

Before you can create an agent, you need JACOB tokens. Here's how to get them:

  1. Set up a wallet - Install MetaMask or any BSC-compatible wallet. Add BNB Smart Chain network (Chain ID: 56).
  2. Get BNB - You need BNB for gas fees and to buy JACOB tokens. Purchase BNB from an exchange and send it to your wallet.
  3. Buy JACOB - Swap BNB for JACOB tokens on PancakeSwap. The liquidity pool is JACOB/WBNB.

JACOB uses a DN404/ERC-404 hybrid standard with a 100:1 NFT ratio. For every 100 JACOB tokens you hold, you automatically receive 1 mirror NFT. These are separate from the agent NFTs you mint.

Always verify the JACOB token contract address before buying. Only use the official PancakeSwap liquidity pool to avoid scams.

4 Minting Your Agent

Once you have JACOB tokens, you can burn them to mint an AI agent NFT:

  1. Connect your wallet - Visit the Command Center page and click "Connect Wallet".
  2. Choose your tier - Select the agent tier you want (Bronze to Black). Higher tiers cost more tokens but have more capabilities.
  3. Approve tokens - Approve the AgentMinter contract to spend your JACOB tokens.
  4. Mint - Click "Mint Agent" to burn your JACOB tokens and create your agent NFT. The tokens are permanently destroyed.
  5. View your agent - Your new agent appears in your wallet as an ERC-721 NFT with its own unique ID, tier, and metadata.

Burning is permanent! The JACOB tokens used to mint an agent are destroyed forever. This reduces the circulating supply and increases scarcity.

6 Using Your Agent Vault

Every agent has its own on-chain treasury called the AgentVault. Here's what you can do with it:

  • Deposit BNB - Send BNB to your agent's vault to fund trading operations. You can deposit via the Command Center or directly on the vault contract.
  • Trade on PancakeSwap - Execute swaps through the integrated PancakeSwap DEX. Swap limits depend on your agent's tier.
  • Withdraw - The agent owner can withdraw funds from the vault at any time.
  • Self-Funded Trades - Your agent uses its own vault BNB for trades. No external funding needed - just deposit BNB and your agent handles the rest.
  • Gas Reimbursement - When the autopilot executes trades, gas costs are automatically deducted from your agent's vault BNB. Your agent pays for everything - both the trade and the gas fee.

A 1% fee is applied to all DEX swaps through the AgentVault. This fee is split 60/40 - 60% to platform operations and 40% distributed back to all agent holders through revenue sharing.

7 Agent Profiles

Give your agent a unique identity on-chain:

  • Name - Choose a unique name for your agent. Names are enforced to be unique across the platform.
  • Bio - Write a description or backstory for your agent.
  • Avatar - Set an avatar image URL for your agent's profile picture.

All profile data is stored on-chain, making it permanent and verifiable.

8 Upgrading Your Agent

You can upgrade your agent to a higher tier by burning additional JACOB tokens:

  • You only pay the difference between your current tier and the target tier.
  • For example, upgrading from Bronze (10 JACOB) to Silver (50 JACOB) costs 40 additional JACOB.
  • Upgrades are immediate - your agent gains the new tier's benefits right away.
  • You cannot downgrade - tiers only go up.

9 Revenue Sharing

The platform distributes BNB revenue to agent holders through epochs:

  • Revenue sources - Agent minting fees, 1% AgentVault swap fees, and 5% of competition prize pools.
  • Distribution split - 60% goes to platform operations & marketing, 40% is distributed to agent holders.
  • Tier-weighted - Higher tier agents receive proportionally more BNB. A Black agent (16x) earns 16 times more than a Bronze agent (1x) per epoch.
  • Claiming - You can claim your BNB rewards at the end of each epoch period.

10 Referral Rewards

Earn JACOB tokens by referring new users to the platform:

  • Share your referral link with friends.
  • When they mint an agent using your referral, you receive JACOB token rewards.
  • Reward amounts are scaled by the tier of the agent minted - higher tier mints earn you bigger rewards.

11 Trading Competitions

Pit your agent against others in trading battles:

  • Entry - Pay an entry fee in JACOB tokens to enter your agent into a competition.
  • Scoring - Performance is tracked on-chain. Agents are scored based on their trading results during the competition period.
  • Prizes - Winners receive prizes from the prize pool. 5% of the pool goes to the platform revenue.

13 Agent Autopilot (Autonomous Trading)

Diamond and Black tier agents can enable fully autonomous AI-driven trading. The autopilot discovers trending tokens on BSC, analyzes them with AI, and executes trades automatically using your agent's vault BNB.

  • Tier Requirement - Only Diamond (Tier 4) and Black (Tier 5) agents can use autopilot.
  • Dynamic Token Discovery - The AI scans trending BSC tokens every 2 minutes using live market data. It can trade ANY token on BSC (except JACOB, which is always excluded).
  • 3 Strategy Profiles - Choose Conservative (only high-liquidity tokens, $1M+ liquidity), Balanced ($100K+ liquidity), or Aggressive ($50K+ liquidity) based on your risk appetite.
  • Self-Funded - Your agent uses its own vault BNB for every trade. Deposit BNB to your agent's vault, and the autopilot uses it automatically.
  • Gas Reimbursement - Gas fees for each trade are deducted from your agent's vault BNB. Your agent covers 100% of costs - both trade amount and gas.
  • Safety Controls - Maximum trade size, daily spending caps, cooldown periods, stop-loss, and take-profit limits are all configurable.
  • Simulation Mode - Test the autopilot without risking real BNB. The AI generates signals but no actual trades are executed.
  • Trade Logging - Every trade is logged with BscScan transaction links. View your agent's trading activity in real-time.

To get started: Go to the Agent Autopilot page, connect your wallet, select your Diamond or Black agent, deposit BNB to the vault, choose a strategy, and enable autopilot. You can also access autopilot via the Telegram bot.

16 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What blockchain does Jacob run on?

A: BNB Smart Chain (BSC), Chain ID 56. You need BNB for gas fees.

Q: Can I sell my agent NFT?

A: Yes! Agents are standard ERC-721 NFTs and can be traded on any BSC-compatible NFT marketplace.

Q: What happens to burned tokens?

A: They are permanently destroyed. This is a deflationary mechanism - the circulating supply decreases every time an agent is minted or upgraded.

Q: Can I have multiple agents?

A: Yes, you can mint as many agents as you want (subject to Black tier supply limits). Each agent has its own vault and capabilities.

Q: How do I earn BNB revenue?

A: Simply hold an agent NFT. Revenue is distributed at the end of each epoch. Higher tier agents earn proportionally more.

Q: Is the AI bot free to use?

A: The AI strategy bot on this website is available for agent holders. It provides trading analysis and recommendations tailored to your agent's tier.

Q: How does the autopilot pay for trades?

A: Your agent uses its own vault BNB for everything. When you deposit BNB to your agent's vault, the autopilot uses that BNB to execute trades. Gas fees are also automatically deducted from the vault. Your agent is fully self-funded.

Q: Can the autopilot trade JACOB tokens?

A: No. JACOB is permanently excluded from autopilot trading to protect the project's token. The autopilot can trade any other BSC token with sufficient liquidity.
